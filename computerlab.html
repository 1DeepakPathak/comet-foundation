Need for computer labs in remote towns

We have adopted few under privileged schools in Pithoragarh district of Uttarakhand, where COMET consultant comes to teach computer basics to the students. Since these schools are lacking the computer labs so we see the pressing need for computer laboratories. Hence, we want to establish a shared computer lab for these schools where students can come in early morning and evening hours to practice their computer lessons.

Request for support:

Establishing such shared computer labs requires financial support, hence we are open to donation from government or private organizations to address this need. Based on our estimation, it requires 4 Lakhs INR to setup a computer lab and run it for one year. From second year onward, it will require approximately 2.5 lakhs INR

For transparency, Organization/donor’s contribution will be updated in COMET website. Thus, organization will know the complete details about computer lab that has been sponsored by them. If donor/organization want, we will be glad to welcome them to the facility and honor them to address the students.
