COMET Mentorship

Considering the challenges that students from remote areas face, we have started the COMET MENTORSHIP PROGRAM to help the students in the following ways –

  1. Helping the students in their education and career development.

  2. Creating opportunity for the COMET students who are doing BCA and other degree courses, with online tuition options.


  1. Program’s objective :closely guide/mentor all students in their education/career development.

  2. Scope : for Class 5 to class 12 students

  3. Program mode : online and study centre based.

  4. Program’s objective : closely guide/mentor all students in their education/career development.

  5. Scope : for Class 5 to class 12 students

  6. Program mode : online and study centre based.

  7. Benefits to the students :

    1. Help students with Maths and science coaching classes.
    2. Spoken English classes for all
    3. Basic computer classes for all.
    4. Career planning and development sessions
    5. Help students to prepare for competitive exams


Request for support:

While we have been mentoring students who want to pursue higher education after 10+2, we want to extend the program for students from class 5 to 12. Our expansion plan is constrained due to financial reasons as we need to onboard and pay the tutor. Hence, in order to onboard more tutors, we would want to come in contact with IT experts and organizations that can assist this cause through a tax-deductible donation or CSR funding.

The donation amount to onboard one additional tutor is approximately 1.5L for one year. For transparency, organization/donor will know the details about tutors who been onboarded due to their sponsorship and the batch details.
