COMET Adopt-a-School

The future of a nation depends upon the quality of education imparted to our children. We believe that it is the joint responsibility of the Government and citizens to improve school education, hence we have begun the process of transforming underprivileged schools by the active involvement of donors, non-government organizations, and corporate sectors through the “COMET – Adopt-A-School Programme”.

This program is an initiative to develop partnerships between schools, professionals, and organizations. The focus of the program is on identifying and solving problems that affect the quality of education and is grounded in the belief that government, corporates, and individuals can all play a pivotal role in accelerating student and school success.

Who can adopt/Contribute?

We are seeking partners who wish to contribute with tax-deductible donations or CSR, like:

  • A public or private sector company

  • A social organization

  • An individual

We earnestly request you to extend your helping hand in making quality school education a reality in India. If you are interested in extending a helping hand in the Adopt-A-School Programme, please contact:

Implementation Strategy:

The following strategy is considered in this program:

  1. Identify the underprivileged schools in rural areas.

  2. Reach out to the donors who volunteer to support the cause.

  3. Assign the schools to COMET consultants who execute the program in schools.

  4. Consultants visit the schools and execute the execution plan.

  5. COMET management reviews the progress status with consultants.

  6. Progress report is shared with the school management.

Planned actions & implementation plan:

The following improvement plan is implemented in each identified school.

  • Session plan/events at school level

  • Motivational sessions of the students by professionals from different industries.

  • Hold a seminar for the parents of the students to educate and inspire them to actively participate in ensuring the success of their children's careers.

  • Supporting/facilitating literary and scientific activities in the school through improved lab and library facilities.

  • Hold inter-school competitions to foster a healthy competition among schools and pupils, and honor the top students (identify star student of the year from each school).

  • Conduct COMET - career fest common to all schools.

Programme Benefits:

“Adopt-a-school fosters a better sense of understanding of the community's school system, strengthens and improves school programs and curricula, and creates a sense of personal involvement and interaction between professionals, organizations, and schools.” Adopt-a-school program is a win-win for everyone. The benefits of adoption include:

  • Improved quality education

  • Career counseling and motivation by experts/professionals.

  • Basic computer sessions

  • Sessions by IT professionals to improve professional and leadership qualities

  • Inter-school competitions to develop a healthy competitive environment.

  • Annual career fest to spread awareness about different career options.

  • Improved school-community relations

  • Support from a highly educated workforce

Request for Support:

We are seeking donations from both government and private organizations to adopt more schools. We also welcome individual contributions with tax-deductible donations. Donation to adopt one school will be approximately 25K per year. For transparency, the organization/donor can know from COMET website which school they have adopted. Even though we have identified schools in Uttarakhand, Bihar, and Assam to execute this program, but we are open to adopt schools based on donor’s preference if they have any.
